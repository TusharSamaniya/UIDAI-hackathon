## Recommendation 1: Priority District Intervention

**Target:** Low coverage & low growth districts
**Problem:** Persistent enrolment stagnation
**Action:** Launch district-specific enrolment drives using mobile units
**Expected Impact:** Rapid improvement in Aadhaar coverage

**Priority:** Immediate (0–6 months)

---

## Recommendation 2: Rural Access Expansion

**Target:** Rural PIN codes
**Problem:** Limited physical access to enrolment centers
**Action:** Deploy mobile enrolment vans and temporary camps
**Expected Impact:** Improved last-mile access

**Priority:** Immediate (0–6 months)

---

## Recommendation 3: Child Enrolment Integration

**Target:** Children aged 0–5 years
**Problem:** Low early-age enrolment
**Action:** Integrate Aadhaar enrolment with Anganwadi and vaccination programs
**Expected Impact:** Early inclusion and long-term coverage stability

**Priority:** Short-term (6–18 months)

---

## Recommendation 4: State-Level Capacity Enhancement

**Target:** Low-performing states
**Problem:** Regional inequality
**Action:** Allocate additional enrolment centers and staff
**Expected Impact:** Reduced interstate enrolment gap

**Priority:** Short-term (6–18 months)

---

## Recommendation 5: Shift to Gap-Based Strategy

**Target:** National UIDAI strategy
**Problem:** Approaching enrolment saturation
**Action:** Move from volume-based to gap-based enrolment planning
**Expected Impact:** Efficient resource utilization

**Priority:** Long-term (18+ months)
